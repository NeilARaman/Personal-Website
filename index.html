Bio

I'm Neil, a first-year student at Carnegie Mellon University majoring in Information Systems and Economics. My academic journey combines coursework with research projects shown below, while my professional experience includes internships at tango and CareOne Medical. On campus, I'm involved with Carnegie Mellon Racing, ScottyLabs, the Business Technology Group, and the Badminton Club to name a few.

Tech-wise, I'm a builder, love going to hackathons, and have a passion for developing AI/ML applications. As demonstrated below, I have experience in libraries like PyTorch and TensorFlow, and can also work full-stack in React and Node. Ultimately, my goal is build software that will truly have an impact, and for one would love to catch more rockets with huge chopsticks.
